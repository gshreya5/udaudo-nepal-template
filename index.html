OUR VISION

If every person is included the world is more connected.

Our mission is to build the capacity of every individual in order to achieve their fullest potential and thereby bring positive change in the society where they live.

Our core businesses are: raising generation of socially responsible youth through transformative education; providing platform for youth engagement through social impact projects.

School Peace Initiative and Campus Peace Initiative


The project focuses on providing life skills support to group of students in schools and colleges. Primary goal is to bring and explore innovative ideas and gain valuable skills.

Activities are created in such a way that youth are ready to succeed in the twenty first century life’s opportunities and challenges. Life skills teaching program i.e. personality skills and character development , creative and critical thinking, positive attitude, self-awareness, Interpersonal relationship, effective communication, decision making, problem solving, coping with emotion, stress management, goal setting and time management, and leadership capacity are being taught as important part for the education of the whole child to instill positive behavior and good character among secondary schools youth and college students.

LEADIn


This project gathers young people for the development of leadership and soft skills ability. The participants join in a 2-day workshop whereby various activities from team building, small group discussions, and individual presentations are held. The project aim to facilitate the success of many young people in both their personal and professional life.

As many graduate students are so focused on developing expertise in curricular subject in their field that they may neglect to realize how important it is to actively work on skills like communication, time management, or conflict resolution. These skills are critical in the workplace and being able to demonstrate them can really help a student stand out in today’s competitive environment.

Udaudo Yuwa Peace club - Play for Peace


The program’s agenda is to promote inclusion and remove the barrier of discrimination. The communities are facing discrimination on grounds of gender and caste that also includes the problem of being insecure and lack of confidence among children and young adolescence.

SMILE (Sharing Meaningful Initiatives on Leadership and Empowerment)


is an exchange program between Nepal and Vietnam. The exchange aims at engaging youth as young leaders to be involved in local and international development issues and to take leadership roles in the organizations as well as within wider society to advocate for positive changes. It has a strong focus on peer education promoting leadership, volunteerism, peace building and community development.

The experiences gained while on exchange are then brought back to the fellow’s home country wherein they implement and contribute their learning experiences.

Udaudo Yuwa Camp


is an experiential learning program. A 3-day series of character and team building activities that help equip the participants with the skills and vision necessary to inspire positive change within themselves and their community. The leadership skills learned is then brought into practice during service projects for the local community.

The rigorous program seeks to equip participants not only with volunteering and leadership skills such as the ability to work in a team, problem solving and adaptability, planning and prioritizing of work, public speaking, program organization, resource development and management, but also character competencies such as relationship building, confidence, and practical application of values.

World Without Borders


as part of Udaudo Nepal’s objective to instill an ethos of respect for diversity. This program creates a common platform for young group of people to share their mutual concern on present scenario of the society, the country, and the world as a whole.

In this program, not only the youth profile of Nepal is being discussed, but also the important role of the youth for diversity is being emphasized. Since the program design is to make use of webinar, the activity does not involve local youth alone but also engage the participation of other young people from different nation around the globe.
